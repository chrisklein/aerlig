Aerlig currently has 43 senior professionals interested in making venture capital investments in high growth potential companies. Senior professionals in our VC group are located in 18 US states, the UK, Canada, South Africa, and Pakistan. All Aerlig senior professionals have successfully run a business of significant size as a President, CEO, or equivalent, and bring deep industry and operational experience to their portfolio companies. The VC group's senior professionals have substantial capital should a company require multiple rounds of financing. Most initial VC group investments are between $1 million and $5 million, but could be larger if the situation is appropriate.

 Industries of interest.
Aerlig's senior professionals prefer to invest in industries where they have direct experience, and in those industries that they believe will experience greater than average growth. Industries of specific interest include:

  • healthcare
  • renewable energy and green technology
  • social media
  • communications technology
  • business and financial services
  • software as a service and cloud computing
  • education technology
 Investing criteria.
To qualify for consideration by the Aerlig VC group, a candidate company should be seeking at least $1 million of financing, and the amount of capital raised should be sufficient to fund the company at its planned burn rate for at least the next 18 months. Candidate companies must also have a business plan that provides sufficient detail for a sophisticated investor to understand all of the major aspects of the company, the management team, and the market in question. Please also provide details about the current ownership structure and preferences for the structure of the new investment.
 How to seek a venture capital investment through Aerlig.
Please submit the candidate company's business plan electronically to plans@aerlig.com. All attachments in submissions should be in either Adobe PDF or a Microsoft Office compatible file format. Submissions will be reviewed and matched with potential venture investors based on industry, capital, and geographic factors. We will treat your business plan in accordance with our privacy policy and terms of use. If you have additional confidentiality questions or concerns, please contact Aerlig by phone at (720) 722-3030 prior to submitting your business plan.
