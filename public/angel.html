Aerlig's angel network consists of 24 senior professionals located in fifteen different US states. Each member of the Aerlig Angel Investor Network is interested in investing in high potential seed stage companies. In addition, each of them has successfully run a significant size business as a President, CEO, or equivalent, and brings far more than capital to the table when they choose to partner with an entrepreneur. Together, the network's senior professionals have substantial capital, and could invest up to $30 million in a single transaction. However, most Angel Network investments are under $1 million, and involve only one or two members of the network.

 Industries of interest.
Naturally, Aerlig's senior professionals prefer to invest in industries where they have direct experience, and in those industries that they believe will experience greater than average growth. Industries of specific interest include:

  • healthcare
  • communications technology
  • manufacturing
  • business and financial services
  • software as a service and cloud computing
  • education
  • consumer products
  • food, beverage, and restaurants
  • gaming
 Investing criteria.
To qualify for consideration by the Aerlig Investor Network, a candidate company should be seeking less than $1 million of financing, and should be able to reach a milestone in its development with the funds sought (e.g. completion of a working prototype, landing an initial customer, or similar). Candidate companies must also have a business plan that provides sufficient detail for an investor to understand the candidate company's potential market, targeted market share, projected revenue and expenses for at least the next three years, biographies of the current management team and descriptions of planned future hires, anything unique or patentable about the product or service, and the management team's logistical plans to bring the product or service to market.
 How to seek an angel investment through Aerlig.
Please submit the candidate company's business plan electronically to plans@aerlig.com. All attachments in submissions should be in either Adobe PDF or a Microsoft Office compatible file format. Submissions will be reviewed and matched with potential investors in the network based on industry, capital, and geographic factors. We will treat your business plan in accordance with our privacy policy and terms of use. If you have additional confidentiality questions or concerns, please contact Aerlig by phone at (720) 722-3030 prior to submitting your business plan.
