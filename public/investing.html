Many of Aerlig's senior professionals held significant stakes in businesses that they exited, resulting in significant personal liquidity. Others have pooled the resources of other investors, and are ready to make substantial investments in individual companies or properties. Whether you are looking for the seed capital necessary to explore the feasibility of a new business idea, would like to explore a sale/leaseback transaction on property you currently own and occupy, or are hoping to find growth capital for an established business, Aerlig can help.

 Angel Network
Aerlig's senior professionals have significant liquidity available for seed stage companies looking for relatively small amounts of capital (less than $1 million). If you have a high potential idea in technology, healthcare, or communications, Aerlig can help.
 Venture Capital
Aerlig's venture capital group invests in high growth potential companies that have not yet reached profitability. Candidate companies should have a strong management team, a unique product or service, and should participate in a rapidly growing industry sector.
 Buyouts
The senior professionals in Aerlig's buyout group seek investments in established, profitable companies in a wide variety of industry sectors. Management sponsored buyouts, leveraged recapitalizations, and family business transitions are favored situations.
 Real Estate
Many of Aerlig's senior professionals are veteran real estate investors, and are looking for opportunities to make further investments in real estate. Commercial, industrial, and residential situations are all open for consideration.
