Aerlig is a privately held company headquartered in Englewood, Colorado. To request an interview with a member of management or one of our senior professionals, please contact us at press@aerlig.com. Basic facts about the company, along with a historical timeline, are shown below.

 Aerlig Facts
Aerlig was founded in 2008 by Paul Morrison who previously had a long career in private equity, venture capital, and entrepreneurship. Aerlig is headquartered in Englewood, Colorado, but has more than sixty senior professionals based in major cities around the globe. Aerlig specializes in consulting for companies facing critical moments, IT due diligence consulting for third party investors, private equity investing, and, through its "CEO Cache" offers experienced CEOs for urgent and special projects of all types.
 Aerlig Timeline

May 2008 - Aerlig Capital, LLC enters into its first consulting engagement.
December 2008 - Aerlig Capital, LLC makes first private equity investment.
December 2011 - Aerlig Capital, LLC adds its 50th senior professional.
