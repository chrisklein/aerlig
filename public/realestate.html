Aerlig has 12 senior professionals interested in being an equity sponsor for a real estate transaction. Our 12 senior professionals are located in 8 US states and the UK. All are experienced real estate investors and most are looking to invest a minimum of $100,000. Our real estate group is open to considering a variety of projects including vacant land, commercial, and residential opportunities.

 Investing criteria.
To qualify for consideration by the Aerlig real estate group, the investment opportunity must be fully defined in a complete offering memorandum. The memorandum should provide sufficient detail for a sophisticated investor to understand all of the major aspects of the opportunity, the management team, and the geographic market in question. Please also provide details about the current ownership structure and the preferred investment structure.
 How to raise equity for a real estate related opportunity through Aerlig.
Please submit information about the real estate opportunity electronically to plans@aerlig.com. All attachments in submissions should be in either Adobe PDF or a Microsoft Office compatible file format. Submissions will be reviewed and matched with potential real estate investors based on project type, size, and geographic factors. We will treat your information in accordance with our privacy policy and terms of use.
