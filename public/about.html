Aerlig, which is the Danish word for "honest," was founded to help businesses with their most critical moments. Aerlig believes that a tremendous amount of value can be created (or lost) when business owners properly (or improperly) navigate their companies through these critical moments. Aerlig is actively creating a network of current and former business owners and private equity professionals who have successfully navigated their own companies through critical moments multiple times, and is making these uniquely qualified consultants and investors available to business owners facing significant opportunities.

 Where does Aerlig operate?
Aerlig is based in Denver, Colorado, and has senior professionals located in throughout the United States, as well as the United Kingdom, the Netherlands, South Africa, Australia, and Pakistan.
 Aerlig's mission is to:
Provide uncolored, actionable advice to business owners and chief executives.

Help maximize the value of all of our client businesses.

Always keep our incentives aligned with those of our clients.

Always behave professionally.

Uphold the strictest standards of confidentiality.

Strive to do the right thing for our clients, employees, and business partners.

Build a great firm.
