 Aerlig's global reach.
Although most of our senior professionals are in the United States, we also have presences in the United Kingdom, The Netherlands, South Africa, Pakistan, and Australia. In addition to providing advice and capital to local firms, our senior professionals also help non-local companies who are looking to expand either their distribution or their supply chains. In addition, many of our United States based senior professionals have extensive overseas connections, and are happy to help with projects requiring an international scope.
